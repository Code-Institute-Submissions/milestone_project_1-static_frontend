Podcasts

The following are podcasts that I listen to on a weekly basis to learn from the guests' experience that they bring on as well as listening to interesting market ideas.

Chat with Traders

This podcast is great for hearing how people started out trading and what to watch out for as a beginner.

The Investors Podcast

This is a great 'value investing' podcast where they have a great quarterly markets sync with their mastermind group.

Macro Voices

This podcast is great for a weekly update about macro-economics and how it is impacting the stock market and currencies.

Top Traders Unplugged

This is a great podcast to get the trend following, systems trading, perpective on the market.
