Recommended Reading

These are the books that I learned a lot from in the last 4 years and allowed me to gain many valuable insights and many different perspectives.

One Good Trade

This book breaks down the process and interactions really well for planning, executing and managing any trade.

Market Wizards

This is a classic and really proves & emphasises that you can carve out profit anywhere in the market and find you edge in anything.

How I Made $2,000,000 in the Stock Market

This book is also a classic and underlines that you can be anyone as long as you apply a working method consistantly.

Top Ten Trading Setups

This book is a great starter book on trading-setups and how to look for them. It also emphasises the importance of keeping it simple.
