Ticker: WTI - Reason Why?


Macro Economics The current pandemic has caused a lack of people going out, spending money, and being around large groups of people. The biggest opportunities for this are the travel industry such as cruise ships and airlines. What do both of these industries use a lot of...oil.

Trade Idea '200 Day call option on WTI' - As I am currently both selling puts and buying puts on the market going down during the pandemic, I thought it might be worth hedging my position with some "insurance" in a substancial call postion for a long period of time that I can sell whenever the a)recovery pump happens unexpectedly, b) a cure gets announced hopefully soon, or c) the oil industry gets bailed out by congress the same way the banks did in 2008, which is even more likely since the US will not want to lose one of their biggest oil companies. Financials You can see from the financials below that the company was profitable before the pandemic hit; The financial forecasting of not being able sell their oil during the pandemic has driven price very low in recent months as you can see on the chart above. The liabilities section of the balance sheet also shows no major debt which would hinder quick recovery.
